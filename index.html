PHASE 1

Choose Your Path

ROS vs. SSM

The first hurdle is choosing the right legal vessel for your mission.

Persatuan (Society)

Via ROS (Registrar of Societies). Recommended for first-time community groups. Access eROSES for easy online registration.

CLBG / Yayasan

Via SSM. For major institutional grants, property ownership, or large-scale corporate foundations.

PHASE 3

Financial Fortress

Banking & Trust

Signatory Protocol

Never allow one-person control. Use 2 or 3 signatories for all transactions. Required: President, Secretary, and Treasurer signatures.

Under RM500 Treasurer + Program Lead
RM500 - RM3,000 President + Treasurer
Above RM3,000 Full Committee Approval
PHASE 3

Launch & Scale

The 12-Month Impact

Month 1 - 3: The Foundation

Register NGO, open bank account, recruit 20 volunteers, and launch 1 pilot project with documentation.

Month 4 - 6: Traction

Publish your first impact report. Document receipts, photos, and beneficiary growth. Recruitment expansion.

Year 1: Sustainability

Complete 3-6 programs, build a consistent donor base, and hold your first official AGM.
